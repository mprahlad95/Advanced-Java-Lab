Develop an app for a bank. the user will have the permission to register with the app. Once registered and approved by admin, the user should be able to login using his credentials. Consider the scenario of not less than 10 users and each one should be able to see their balance. In case of any transactions, made to any other account, show the current balance after the transaction. The admin should have the provision to block any account. The users should have a provision to see their transactions based on selected dates.
